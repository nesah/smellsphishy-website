About

SMELLSPHISHY is a new way to detect fake websites that try to steal personal information. It enhances the ability to recognize, and block these malicious websites, even those that are cleverly designed to avoid detection to provide better protection for users against threats.

With the vision that started in 2024, four student-developers—Shane Delos Reyes, Jeferson Escueta, Rhabi Mendoza, and Zen Ubungen—pursued and persisted to create an advanced way to protect our information from threat actors. With the aim to contribute to the field of computing, the four student-developers conducted thorough research and study, culminating in the creation of this innovative solution as the very fruit of their bachelor's degree.

Features

SMELLSPHISHY utilizes ensemble machine learning model, Random Forest, allowing effective and efficient classification of URL by analyzing its characteristics. To ensure that it is efficient against carefully crafted phishing URLs attacks, it also employed Genetic Algorithm to simulate the said attacks.
